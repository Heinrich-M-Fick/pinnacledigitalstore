Launch Guide

How to Go Live — Step by Step

Follow these steps exactly. You can be live and taking real payments within 2 hours.

1
Download All Your Files
5 min

Save these 5 files from Claude to a folder on your computer called pinnacle-store:

  • index.html — your website
  • personal-finance-mastery.pdf
  • social-media-content-calendar.pdf
  • business-plan-pro-template.pdf
  • marketing-strategy-playbook.pdf

All files must be in the same folder.

2
Set Up PayFast (Link Your Capitec Account)
20 min

Step 2a: Go to payfast.co.za ↗ and click "Register as Merchant".

Step 2b: Fill in your business details. Under "Bank Account", enter your Capitec Entrepreneur account number and branch code (470010).

Step 2c: Upload your SA ID and proof of bank account (a Capitec bank statement or account confirmation letter).

Step 2d: After approval (usually same day), go to your PayFast dashboard → Settings → Integration and copy your:

  • Merchant ID
  • Merchant Key

Step 2e: Open index.html in a text editor (Notepad or VS Code). Find the line that says PAYFAST_MERCHANT_ID and PAYFAST_MERCHANT_KEY and replace the placeholder values with your real ones.

PayFast pays out to your Capitec account within 2–3 business days of each sale. Fee: 3.5% + R2.00 per transaction.

3
Deploy Your Website Free on Netlify
10 min

Step 3a: Go to netlify.com ↗ and sign up free (use your Google or email account).

Step 3b: On your Netlify dashboard, look for the box that says "Drag and drop your site folder here".

Step 3c: Drag your entire pinnacle-store folder (containing all 5 files) onto that box.

Step 3d: Netlify will give you a free URL like pinnacle-digital.netlify.app. Your site is live immediately.

Step 3e (Optional): Buy a custom domain like pinnacledigital.store from Google Domains ↗ (~R200/year) and connect it in Netlify → Domain Settings.

4
Set Up Gumroad for International Sales (UK & Global)
15 min

For international buyers paying in GBP/USD, use Gumroad alongside PayFast.

Step 4a: Go to gumroad.com ↗ and create a free account.

Step 4b: Upload each PDF as a product. Set prices in USD (the site shows GBP equivalent automatically):

  • Personal Finance Mastery — $12
  • Social Media Content Calendar — $14
  • Business Plan Pro — $15
  • Marketing Strategy Playbook — $17
  • Full Bundle — $28

Step 4c: Copy each product's Gumroad link. On your website, the "Buy Now" buttons for international customers can link to Gumroad. Gumroad pays out to PayPal or Stripe, which connects to your SA bank.

5
Test Everything Before Going Live
10 min
  • Open your live site URL and check all pages load correctly
  • Add a product to cart and go through the checkout flow
  • PayFast has a sandbox test mode — use it to process a test payment first
  • Confirm the download link in the confirmation email works
  • Check the site looks good on your phone (mobile test)
6
Launch — Tell the World
You're live!

Now sell. Here's the fastest path to your first sale:

  • 📱 Post on WhatsApp Status with your store link and what you're selling
  • 📘 Post on Facebook — personal profile and any business groups you're in
  • 📸 Post a Reel/TikTok showing the products and the value inside
  • 💌 Email everyone in your contacts with a personal message
  • 🔗 Add your store link to every social media bio

Most first sales come from people you already know. Don't wait for strangers to find you — tell your network first. One WhatsApp message to 50 contacts can generate 3–5 sales today.

Payments

PayFast + Capitec Setup Guide

Complete guide to linking PayFast to your Capitec Entrepreneur account and accepting payments in ZAR and internationally.

🏦 Why PayFast for South Africa

PayFast is South Africa's most trusted payment gateway. It accepts credit/debit cards, Instant EFT, SnapScan, Mobicred, and all major SA bank transfers — and pays directly into your Capitec Entrepreneur account within 2–3 business days. Setup is free.

1
Register: Go to payfast.co.za → Register as Merchant → Complete your business profile
2
Bank Details: Enter your Capitec Entrepreneur account number. Branch code: 470010. Upload a bank statement or Capitec confirmation letter as proof.
3
Verification: Submit your South African ID. PayFast verifies within hours to 1 business day.
4
Get Your Keys: In your PayFast dashboard → Settings → Integration. Copy your Merchant ID and Merchant Key. Keep these private.
5
Update Your Site: In index.html, find the PayFast config section and replace the placeholder values with your real Merchant ID and Key.
6
Test First: PayFast provides a sandbox environment. Test a full payment before going live. Dashboard: payfast.co.za → Integration → Sandbox.

🌍 International Sales (UK & Global)

For UK and international customers, use Gumroad (free) or Stripe (more technical). Gumroad is the simplest: upload your PDFs, set USD prices, and international buyers pay with any card. Gumroad deposits to PayPal, which you can withdraw to your SA bank account.

  • Gumroad fee: 10% per sale (no monthly fee)
  • Stripe fee: 2.9% + $0.30 (requires more setup)
  • Both pay out in USD/GBP which converts to ZAR when withdrawn
